Compute eigenvalues and optionally eigenvectors of matrix A.

Compute for an N-by-N complex nonsymmetric matrix A, the eigen- values and, optionally, right eigenvectors

See zgeev.

Parameters
[in]Areal matrix
[in]wlist containing the eigenvalues of A
[in]vrif vr != NULL, right eigenvectors are computed and will be stored in vr; vr must be a complex matrix of dimension (dim,1) (i.e. a vector)
[in]vlif vl != NULL, lefft eigenvectors are computed and will be stored in vl; vl must be a complex matrix of dimension (dim,1) (i.e. a vector)
Return values
0on success

Compute eigenvalues and optionally eigenvectors of Hermitian matrix A.

This function computes all eigenvalues and, optionally, eigenvectors of a Hermitian symmetric matrix A.

See zheev.

Parameters

[in]JOBZ'N': only eigenvalues, 'V' eigenvalues and eigenvectors
[in]UPLO'U': upper triangle part of A is stored; 'L': lower triangle part of A is stored
[in]wreal matrix of dimension (dim,1) (i.e. a vector); the eigenvalues will be stored in w
